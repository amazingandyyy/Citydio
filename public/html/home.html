Citydio Turn a city into a audiobook

A city exploration app built in IoT for Cities Hackathon.


Functions

  • Pin your location from any devices
  • check details and story of that location
  • have a photo generated by Predix APIs
  • Have a quote from Oxford Project
  • Have alexa to read the quote for you
  • Give feed backm explore more, help more.

APIs applied

  • GE Predix
  • Pitney Bow
  • Amazon Alexa
  • Microsoft Project Oxford
